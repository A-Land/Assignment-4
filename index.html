History of Steve Wozniak

Steve Wozniak was born August 11, 1950 in San Jose California. He is commonly know as Woz. He was an undisciplined student and dropped out of several colleges. In 1971 he designed the Blue Box that allow people to hack into the telephone network without paying for long distance calls.
That's where he met Steve Jobs, a student from his old high school.They started selling the Blue Box to other students. While working as an engineering intern at Hewlett-packard, Wozniak designed his owned micro computer, but the company was not interested in his design. Wozniak and Jobs decided to work together to form a company Apple Computer and they set up production in the jobs family garage to build micro circuit boards

Steve Wozniak Contributions

Steve Wozniak was a big part of Apple
he and Steve Jobs started Apple in April 1, 1976. 1977 completed the Apple 2, which included a build-in keyboard support for a color monitor, and designed new hardware components, such as the three point 5 inch floppy disc drive, and various components of the Apple operating system.
