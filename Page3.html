Apple computers

Apple Inc. — originally known as Apple Computers — began in 1976. Apple first started in Steve Jobs garage in Los Altos, California.


On April 1, 1976, they debuted the Apple 1, a desktop computer that came as a single motherboard, pre-assembled, unlike other personal computers of that era.


As Apple was increasing in popularity other companies entered the market space. Those companies included:

  • Samsung
  • HP Computers
  • IBM
  • Acer
